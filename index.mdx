### I.2 Global SPEI database

- <http://spei.csic.es/database.html>
- Time scale: 1-month to 48-month
- Time range: 1901--2015 (Downloadable) / 1955--2018 (Online)
- Spatial resolution: 0.5 degree
- Format: NetCDF
